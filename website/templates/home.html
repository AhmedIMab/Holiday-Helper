What we do!

The primary objective of Holiday helper is to help those unsure of where they want to travel to, we do this by:

• Asking you various questions to better understand the type of holiday you are looking for

• Using your responses to create a personalised score system for the different factors we feature

• Using these 'factor scores' to then compare with all the countries in the world and see which one closely matches your preference

For example, if you answer yes to a question about winter activities and give us a rough estimate of your budget, we will recommend countries which are good for winter activities like skiing which match your budget
