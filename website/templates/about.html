About Holiday Helper!

Holiday helper started off as a personal project with the aim of helping people decide on the best place to travel based on the type of holiday they are looking for.

When using the app, please remember the following:

  • Although Holiday Helpers uses real data from various sources, please note that the data isn't perfect and whilst the goal is to help you as much as possible, we recommend checking locations thoroughly before committing to the destination.
  • Our goal is to give you the most accurate information, however the data at the current time is up to date as of 2021
  • If interested in the legal and data information, please go to the legal page in the footer of the site.
