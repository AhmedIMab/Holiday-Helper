About Holiday Helper!

Holiday helper started off as a personal project with the aim of helping people decide on the best place to travel based on a vague idea of the holiday they are looking for!

When using the app, please remember the following:

  • Although Holiday Helper uses real data from various sources, please note that the data isn't perfect and whilst the goal is to help you as much as possible, we recommend checking locations thoroughly before committing to the destination.
  • Our goal is to give you the most accurate information, however the data at the current time is up to date as of 2021 but does vary based on the source. Data used from external sources is mentioned below.
  • Holiday Helper is not affiliated with or endorsed by any of the organizations or data providers mentioned below.

Privacy Policy

Quick note, this is still a small project in very early stages, and our main goal is to build an app that actually helps users. We only ask for your name, email, and password so that you can create and use your account. Your password is always stored securely and never in plain text. We don’t track you or collect anything else. Your info is only used to make the app work. If you have any questions about your data, you can reach us via support@holidays-helper.com

As we are slowly working on improving Holiday Helper, any feedback is much appreciated Please feel free to mention anything you think might be useful!
